About us

Theism Elevators was established in 1989, by Bengali entrepreneur R. N. Saha.
It was born as a division of Theism Engineering Co., the second company established under the aegis of Theism Group.

In its early years, the company was engaged in erection and maintenance of elevators for the Garrison Engineers - serving military installations across the Eastern Command. It was also involved in projects commissioned by the Government of India and the West Bengal Public Works Department. Theism Elevators grew into a primary choice contractor for AmbujaNeotia projects, Bengal Srishti, Team Taurus and other large-scale private sector enterprises.
